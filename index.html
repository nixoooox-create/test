Since 2019

Cooked over real fire, plated without ceremony.

Ember & Salt started as a single charcoal grill on a rooftop in Bab El Oued. Everything on the menu still passes over open flame — sea bream, lamb shoulder, charred vegetables — finished with citrus, olive oil, and whatever herbs came in that morning. No tasting menu, no pretense. Just fire, salt, and good company.
